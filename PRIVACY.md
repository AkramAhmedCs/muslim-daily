# Privacy Policy for Muslim Daily

**Effective Date:** January 2026

## Overview

Muslim Daily is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your religious practices are personal and should remain private.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect any data.**

Muslim Daily:
- Does NOT collect personal information
- Does NOT collect usage analytics
- Does NOT collect location data
- Does NOT require user registration
- Does NOT transmit any data to external servers

## Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device:
- Daily checklist progress
- Adhkar repetition counts
- App preferences and settings
- Notification schedules

This data never leaves your device.

## Network Access

Muslim Daily does NOT make any network requests. All Islamic content (adhkar, hadith, Quran) is bundled with the app and available offline.

## Third-Party Services

We do NOT use any third-party services such as:
- Analytics platforms
- Advertising networks
- Social media integrations
- Cloud storage services

## Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| Notifications | To send adhkar reminders (optional) |

You can deny these permissions and the app will still function fully in manual mode.

## Children's Privacy

Muslim Daily does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, changes will be included in the app update notes.

## Contact

For privacy concerns or questions:
- Create an issue on our GitHub repository

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete.

---

**Summary:** Muslim Daily is a fully offline, privacy-respecting app that collects zero data. Your religious practices remain completely private.
